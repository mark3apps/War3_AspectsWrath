Description

From the Halls of Anguish inside the Black Temple comes a Fel Orc, never seen anywhere else like this:

The Fel Orc Behemoth.
A heavy melee unit for the Fel Orcs to take on the heavy units of the other armies in Outland.

I drew, clearly, inspiration from the Bonechewer Behemoths,
back then in WoW: The Burning Crusade.
I just wanted to make them a little more "suitable for the masses"
and that they fit into the overall look of the Fel Horde, but there are some small details built in to connect them to their original 13 years older.

Update, January 3rd: I added a green version upon request.
