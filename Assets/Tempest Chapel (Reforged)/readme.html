Description

Tempest Chapel trains Strike Angels, Conjurers and Valkyries. Can also contains spell upgrades for Strike Angels and Conjurers, Laud Hailers and Magic Sentry Upgrades.

The Tempest Chapel is home to the Sisterhood's elite mages, they train young sorceresses to learn advanced magic to succeed and how to cast it correctly or wrongly. Even in their adolescent age, they know how to cast it properly in the heat of battle.
