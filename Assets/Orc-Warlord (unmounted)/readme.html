Description

"Death to all who oppose the Horde!"

In the past, the chieftain of an orc clan was also the warlord of the same.
But it could also happen that the Warchief of the Horde named a Slayer who performed great deeds on behalf of the Horde as a warlord and was thus granted control of an orc clan.
Either way, a warlord leads his clan's armies by example. In the first row, there where the ground is covered with blood, shouting war cries, he hacks his way through every land-based resistance with beastly strength.

Actually, I just wanted to get the orc warlord off his horse, but then decided against it because of the really ugly texturing and built my own from scratch.
Thanks to Bagysta for letting me use his texture and a big thank you to Archaos, who helped me with words and deeds to give the warlord a new animation set and how to fix glitching geosets!
Thank you very much.
